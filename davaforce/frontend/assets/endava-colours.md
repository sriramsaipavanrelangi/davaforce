## Usage Notes

- Core brand expression leans heavily on Red Orange, Solid Blue, and Pure White.
- Solid Blue and Pure White are used for text and backgrounds.
- Red Orange is used sparingly for accents and highlights.
- Text is generally Solid Blue on Pure White or Pure White on Solid Blue.
- Solid Blue shades can support hierarchy in digital applications.
